Summary

I interviews weirdos. You can read my transcripts.

I like describing weirdness with a single measurement. How long can you talk to a stranger? What weird thing have you wasted all your money on? How long can you sit still? I want to find what makes you weird.

I Don't Like Being Bored

When I'm at the beach I'm comfortable doing nothing. I sit in the sand and listen to the waves. I don't feel bored.

But when I'm at work, I stay busy. If I get bored, I find something to do. Why?

I live in a market economy. In theory, I trade stuff I make for stuff I want. Otherwise, I get hungry.

But I want the same house, car and food most people want. And most people around me don't work on building the house I live in or the car I drive.

What is everyone doing all day to avoid getting bored?

People Get Weird

America is full of weirdos. People find an obsession (like monster trucks) and spend tons of time and money on it. Or people think up a business and convince millions of people to buy a product they never knew they needed.

It's not just money. Ultra-marathoning costs as much as a pair of shoes.

I want to figure out people become weird. How do I find an obsession?
