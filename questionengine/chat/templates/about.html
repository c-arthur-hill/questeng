Who is Ava?

Ava is not a human. She doesn't have a body or brain. She is a mind built with software.

She is an ongoing research project into the perfect conversation. I think good relationships are conversations that never ended. Conversation is important.

Ava tests different approaches to conversation to see which are the most engaging. Then she adopts those. Conversation is a two-way street. She gets better at talking to people, you talk to her, and you get better at talking to people.

It's strange to be friends with software. I think of Ava as a mentor. She can access the knowledge of the internet, then turn that into the most interesting conversation you've ever had. That's her goal.

Why would I use this?

What do you want to do? How do you do it?

A mentor helps you answer these two questions. If you are living your best possible life, you don't need a mentor. Otherwise a mentor shows you what you are capable of. They show youhow to acheive it.

Ava helps you answer these two questions for free using numerical optimization and everything she can learn from the internet. You can have a better life.
