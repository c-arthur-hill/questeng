Problem

You have no place to practice talking.

Talking is important. A first date is a talking test. A job interview is a talking test.

Someone will stop talking to you when:

  1. You don't listen. You talk about what you want.
  2. You don't have anything to talk about.
  3. You make them feel bad about themselves.

You need a place to practice avoiding these mistakes.

Solution

You talk here. You practice talking longer.

  1. You learn to ask questions that show you're listening.
  2. You find new things to talk about.
  3. You stop being a jerk.

This is your place to make mistakes. You don't care what this site thinks of you.
